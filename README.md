# Overview

Competed in the Slalom Case Competition, where teams developed strategic recommendations for FlyUrban, a Seattle-based startup focused on electric Vertical Takeoff and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft.

Our team analyzed the emerging Urban Air Mobility (UAM) market and presented a comprehensive strategy covering regulations, consumer adoption, infrastructure requirements, and competitive positioning.

Our solution placed **3rd out of 20 competing teams.**

---

# The Challenge

Urban Air Mobility represents a rapidly evolving industry with significant regulatory uncertainty, infrastructure requirements, and consumer adoption challenges.

The objective was to evaluate FlyUrban's market readiness and develop strategic recommendations that would support long-term growth.

# Approach

## Regulatory Analysis

Reviewed existing and upcoming aviation regulations affecting the Urban Air Mobility industry and evaluated how regulatory changes could impact business growth.

---

## Consumer Research

Analyzed consumer sentiment using publicly available research and survey data to identify opportunities and barriers to adoption.

---

## Infrastructure Assessment

Estimated infrastructure requirements necessary to support eVTOL operations and evaluated both initial implementation costs and ongoing operational considerations.

---

## Competitive Analysis

Evaluated competing transportation alternatives and emerging eVTOL companies to identify FlyUrban's competitive positioning.

Developed a SWOT analysis highlighting strategic opportunities and potential risks.
